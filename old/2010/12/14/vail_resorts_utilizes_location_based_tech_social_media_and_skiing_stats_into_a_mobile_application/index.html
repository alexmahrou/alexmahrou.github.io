Vail Resorts utilizes location based tech, social media, and skiing stats into a Mobile Application

14 December 2010 on iphone, android, mobile, lbs, skiing
[![](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/_ST-5TvqsJwM/TQfsFuW03AI/AAAAAAAAAC0/tYAmqwbFxcc/s320/Capture2.PNG)](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/_ST-5TvqsJwM/TQfsFuW03AI/AAAAAAAAAC0/tYAmqwbFxcc/s1600/Capture2.PNG)
_Image Courtesy: skiingmag.com Vail has upped the ante with their new application Epic Mix application.  The application utilizes the preexisting RF-scanners at the base of every lift, the RF tags build into season passes and PEAKS cards.  You can use the smartphone app to track your statistics (vertical feet logged, days skied, locations skied, etc.).  One can assume that this will not only be good for sharing stats with friends, but also a way for families to track the kiddos on the mountain.  _

It will be available at Breckenridge, Keystone, Vail, Beaver Creek, and Heavenly Resorts by Christmas 2010.

Supported Mobile Platforms:

  • Android
  • iPhoneEpicMix
